—ABOUT US—

There are innumerable people, specifically the ones from the Indian Subcontinent , who haven't been able to go home and crave the Indian delicacies. We, here at P.I.N.D , are trying to deliver the taste, spices and desi style with a hint of the atmosphere of our home.

New York - "concrete jungle where dreams of" , one of the busiest cities in the world, also known as the Melting Pot, with people from all around the world residing there was the perfect location for P.I.N.D to highlight the indian cuisine on a global level.

A bunch of college kids working on a school project together came up with this idea and worked towards building it from the grassroot level starting off with this very website.Those prodigious kids are ones whose photos you can see just below this section.
